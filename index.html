ABOUT ME

I am a professor at Yau Mathematical Science Center (YMSC) Tsinghua University in China. I got my bachelor's degree at Tsinghua University in China in 2009. I obtained my Ph.D. at Orsay, France in 2013 under the supervision of Wendelin Werner. Prior to YMSC, as a C.L.E. Moore instructor at MIT in the US from 2013 to 2015 and as a postdoc researcher at NCCR/SwissMAP Geneva University in Switzerland from 2015 to 2017.
